After a decade and a half of working in the interiors industry,designer Meera Pyarelal decided to set up her eponymous brand Temple Town ,with the aim of designing and manufacturing sophisticated pieces of wooden furniture.

The furniture is inspired from antiques,beautifully reimagined for modern living. They are hand made using the same the same crafts skills and materials as the originals.

With Temple Town designer Meera Pyarelal is making a name for herself with a refined take on opulence.

"Change is the only constant, and it is coming soon and it is refined, opulent and unapologetically extravagant", says the designer.
